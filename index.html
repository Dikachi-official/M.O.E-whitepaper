What is Monks of Sui?

Defi protocol on Sui blockchain,Connecting Applications with Blockchain Ecosystems. With several projects built on different blockchain Sui & Ethereum
Monks of Sui is a Defi Protocol native to SUI ecosystem and will provide SUI users with an unparalleled one-stop Defi experience.
Users can choose M.O.S's smart contracts to initiate token-independent liquidity pools and leverage It's proprietary formula to provide fast, low-slippage, low-fee transactions for SUI ecosystem tokens.
In addition, users can take advantage of opening an Araya Credit account to enjoy the leverage services from native Defi primitive.
On top of that, Id services one the one hand allows users to monitor the status of their property in real time and to quickly access market opportunities with the unique benefits of Araya Finance, and on the other hand, provides the DID infrastructure to facilitate Web3 ecology complete the point-to-point mapping of users in the Web3 world and the peripheral world. For developers in the SUI ecosystem, Monks of Sui Finance will provide developers with a one-stop token offering solution, from the offering format to the implementation of the solution.
